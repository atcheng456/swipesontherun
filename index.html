SO HERE ARE THE STATS...

  • 22 million pounds of food are wasted on college campuses anually 
  • Each college student generates 142 pounds in food waste          
  • 41.2 million people face hunger in the United States             

We're taking action to fight food insecurities in the surrounding Tufts community! Join us every week in our mission by getting involved in our food runs or by donating a meal swipe to our food bank. Help us spread the word. Every action makes a difference.
