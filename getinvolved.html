MAKE A DIFFERENCE WITH US

Join us every week on our food runs around the Somerville and Medford community! From 6pm - 7:30pm, we connect with folks in the surrounding area and deliver food collected from the Tufts dining halls. Leading up to each run, students are campaigning for swipe donations to feed people in need.

All you have to do is fill out the form linked below, go to the dining hall on a designated day of your choice, and pack a container of food! We'll be in touch with the specific details!
